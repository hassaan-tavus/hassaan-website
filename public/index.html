ABOUT ME

Howdy, I'm Hassaan Raza. I'm the CEO and Co-Founder of Tavus. Tavus is a generative AI research company building a new human-computer interface using conversational video/digital twins. We like to joke that we were doing generative AI before there was a nice name for it. My background is in computer science, and before Tavus, I was a software engineer for many years as well as a PM (being a SWE was more my vibe though, I like building). I spend most of my time these days in San Francisco at the Tavus office in the Mission (come visit!) working with an insane team.
