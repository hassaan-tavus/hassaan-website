About Hassaan

ABOUT ME

Howdy, I'm Hassaan Raza. I'm the CEO and Co-Founder of Tavus. Tavus is a AI research lab focused on human computing, the pursuit of teaching machines how to be human. My background is in computer science, and before Tavus, I was a software engineer for many years as well as a PM (being a SWE was more my vibe though, I like building). I spend most of my time these days in San Francisco at the Tavus office in the Mission (come visit!) working with an insane team. This website was made for fun, but primarily to hold context for my digital twin.
